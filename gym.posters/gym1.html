Bodybuilding helps improve overall physical strength and muscle development. It enhances metabolism and supports healthy weight management. Regular training boosts mental health by reducing stress and increasing focus. The sport encourages discipline through consistent workouts and proper nutrition.

Arnold Schwarzenegger is one of the most influential bodybuilding icons in history. Ronnie Coleman became famous for winning eight Mr. Olympia titles. Jay Cutler is known for his powerful physique and multiple championship victories. Phil Heath impressed fans with his symmetry, conditioning, and stage presence.
